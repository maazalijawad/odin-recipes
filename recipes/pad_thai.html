Pad Thai

Description

A vibrant and aromatic Thai stir-fry dish, known for its perfect balance of sweet, sour, and savory flavors, topped with fresh garnishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 oz) of package rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 tbsp of white sugar
  • 2 tbsp of fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp of white wine vinegar
  • 1/8 tbsp of crushed red pepper
  • 2 cups of bean sprouts
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of crushed peanuts
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Soak the rice in cold water until soft, 30-50 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  3. heat butter in a wok; add chicken and saut√© until browned. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  4. Heat oil in the wok over medium-high and crack eggs to cook until firm. Stir in the chicken and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the softened noodles, sugar, fish sauce, vinegar, and red pepper; mix well until noodles are tender.
  6. Stir bean sprouts in and cook for 3 minutes.
  7. Serve topped with green onions, crushed peanuts, and a wedge of lemon.
