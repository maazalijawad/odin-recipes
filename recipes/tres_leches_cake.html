Tres Leches Cake

Description

A rich, moist sponge cake soaked in a creamy three-milk mixture and finished with fluffy whipped cream for a decadent treat.

Ingredients

    Cake

  • 1 1/2 cups of all purpose-flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 cups of white sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract

    • Milk Soak

  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1 (14 oz) can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (12 fl oz) can of evaporated milk

    • Whipped Cream

  • 1 1/2 cups of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of white sugar

Steps

    Making the Cake

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9 x 13 inch baking pan.
  3. Sift the flour and baking powder together; set aside.
  4. Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
  5. Mix in eggs and vanilla and beat well.
  6. Add in the flour mixture at intervals of 1/2 cups while mixing each interval until well blended.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for about 30 minutes.
  8. After taking the cake out of the oven, pierce all over the cake with a fork and let it cool to room temperature.

    9. Soaking with Milk Mixture

  9. Mix the whole milk, condensed milk, and evaporated milk in a bowl.
  10. Pour the mixture on top of the cooled cake and let it soak in.

    11. Whipped Cream

  11. Whip the cream, remaining 1 cup of sugar, and remaining 1 tsp of vanilla in a metal bowl until thick.
  12. Spread over top of the cake.
  13. Keep the cake refrigerated until ready to serve and enjoy!
